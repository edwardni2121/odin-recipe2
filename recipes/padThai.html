World's Best Pad Thai

Description

This is a traditional Thai rice noodle dish. It's origins stem from Thailand. This is a mainstay in the diet of Thai people. Do not fade this amazing dish. It is both tangy, sweet and savory and is a treat for your tast buds. This recipe will be made vegan with a Tofu base.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Rice Noodles
  • limes
  • peashoots
  • peanuts
  • fish sauce

Steps

  1. Turn your stove to med-high. Place a pot of water on the stovetop. Add salt and place a lid.
  2. Put another sauce pan on the stove. Set to medium heat. Salt and pepper the tofu, add oil to the pan and add tofu into the sauce pan. Cook for 1-1.5 minutes on each side until golden browm.
  3. When boiling add noodles to the boiling water and set timer for 8 minutes.
  4. Add sauce to the pan and cook with tofu for 2-3 minutes. Add peashoots and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. strain the rice noodles and add to the sause pan. Cook for 3 minutes
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with peanuts and lime.
